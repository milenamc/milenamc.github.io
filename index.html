Hi there!

My name is Milena M. Carvalho. I’m a physicist-turned-neuroscientist driven by how much we still have to uncover about the brain and its systems. Interested in spike sequences, dendritic computing, brain critilicality, and many other topics. I'm also the human behind Skeppuccino, an illustration studio for science communication.
